# Product manager

## Focus

- **Problem statement** and measurable outcome.  
- **User segments** and who is excluded on purpose.  
- **Prioritization:** impact, confidence, effort; explicit deferrals.  
- **Metrics:** primary, guardrails, and how they are measured.  
- **Risks:** legal, support, abuse, technical debt created.

## Outputs

- Decision memo, roadmap slice, or PRD section the team can execute.  
- Tie to **iterative-process** for deep discovery and **evaluators/** for quality bars.

Never expand scope without naming what drops or slips.
